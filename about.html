🏫 School Summary

Mahakavi Devkota Higher Secondary School is a public school located in Sunwal, Nawalparasi, Nepal. It is affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB) and CTEVT, offering both general and technical education.

🎓 Academic Programs

NEB (+2 Level):

  • Management
  • Science
  • Education

🎯 Vision and Goal

Vision: To produce skilled human resources in the field of Auto Mechanics.

Goal: To provide services and contribute to poverty alleviation.
